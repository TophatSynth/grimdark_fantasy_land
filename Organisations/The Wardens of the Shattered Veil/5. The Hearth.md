# Overview

The Hearth is what the [[1. Overview|Wardens of the Shattered Veil ]]call their Keep, their area of permanent control, their fortress and home.

---
## The Fracture and The Hearth

The ancients wards that still protect the Hearth to this day, not only preserve the actual structure from crumbling to dust, but also keep the mutating reality of [[The Mirror & The Veil|The Mirror]] at bay. 

Wardens and any beings residing within the Hearth not only have a significantly stronger resistance to mutations, but the accumulated buildup of Fracture within individuals slowly drains there.
