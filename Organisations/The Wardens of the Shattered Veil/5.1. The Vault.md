# Overview

Located deep within the catacombs beneath the keep, lays the Warden's sanctum of restricted knowledge. Where most scrolls, tomes and notes the Wardens and especially the generations of Lord Librarians have collected over the centuries are available in [[5.2. The Archive|the Archive]] to all curious Members that seek guidance or information, the *Vault* is overseen directly by the current Lord Librarian and access to it is strictly regulated.

Except for rare occasions only [[2.1. The Wardbearers|Wardbearers]] have access as the *Vault* mostly is of use to them in the first place.

---

The Ward has long ago decided that counting on chance and mutation to allow *Wardbearers* to create wards and pierce the [[The Mirror & The Veil|Veil]] was by far to unreliable. Seeing that it was and is paramount to ensure the Ward's continued existence that access to Reality stays ensured, it was decided that the ban on [[Veil-Scarring (Or Essence Weaving)|Essence Weaving]] needed to have exceptions. From that point onward, future Wardbearers during their training had to prove their ability to master the art of *Essence Weaving* to a sufficient degree that allows them to:
- Pierce the Veil
- Detect thin-veiled areas
- Create minor wards
